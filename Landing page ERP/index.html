SERVICES

Master Pro ERP offers the following services and features in its platform.

Financial Auditing Systems

The heart of financial accounting. It manages cash, accounting entries and financial reports.

Auditing & Posting Management is system for auditing accountants, documents on transactions and approved them by conformity, disconformity or rejection.

Managing the organization strategy to work program and ensuring that transactions are carried out correctly to achieve its goals defined in budget.

Human Capital System

Monitoring administrative operations such as (permissions, work duties, tasks …etc.) and approving them before their impact on the actual work hours.

Designed to manage personnel affairs related to procedures, services, and others.

Adapting and configuring system according to the organization’s business nature and staff.

Industiral Administration System

The production resource planning system is a system for managing the industrial companies for the production process cycle in its various stages.

Implementing quality procedures of raw materials and finished products according to the approved quality procedures in the facility.

Monitoring raw materials and all the industrial processes until reaching the final product.
